Roasted Salsa

  • 3-4 Tomatoes
  • 4-5 Tomatillos
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 3-5 cloves of Garlic(chopped)
  • 2-5 Serano peppers(depending on how spicy)
  • 1 cup Cilantro plucked
  • 1-3 teaspoon salt
  1. Preheat Oven: set the oven to Broilhigh
  2. Quarter onion
  3. Arrange all vegetables (except garlic and cilantro) on anat least one inch high oven pan -- coat in vegetable oil
  4. Place Pan in broiler-- or the top shelf of oven with broil setting high
  5. Roast vegetables until they start to blacken -- rotate if necissary
  6. Rest vegetables until cool enough to handle
  7. Remove skin from tomatos and peppers
  8. Collect all vegetables and add them to a food processor or molcajete
  9. Pulse or Mash until desired consitency
  10. Refrigerate until ready to serve!
