Handmade Tortillas

  • 2 cupsmasa harina
  • 1 1/2 to 2 cups hot water
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  1. Mix Dough: in a large mixing bowl, whisk together Masa and salt-- slowly add hot water stiring with wood spoon
    knead dough for two to three minutes until smooth(we are looking for a playdoug like consitency)
  2. Let dough rest: Cover bowl with damp cloth or paper towel
  3. Portion dough: Portion rested dough into golf ball sized spheres.
  4. Press dough:A tortilla press is recommended but if you don't have a tortilla press
    you can use two plates to smash the spheres into flat circles
  5. COOKIN' TIME:Heat a non stick skillet or comal on medium high. once pan is hot
    heat tortilla 40-60 seconds on each side
  6. Serve! the masa has now transformed into an appropriate vessel for tacoy goodness.
